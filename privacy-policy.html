Privacy Policy - Period Tracker Calendar 2K25

Last updated: June 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect and store information you voluntarily provide, such as period start dates, symptoms, ovulation data, sexual activity, and other health-related inputs.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to offer personalized cycle tracking, including period and ovulation predictions, fertility windows, and timely reminders to support your reproductive health.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely either locally on your device or on secure servers (if cloud sync is enabled). We implement safety measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or health data with any third parties, unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time via the app. You may also stop using the app whenever you choose.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the app.
